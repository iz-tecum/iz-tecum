Representation of Riemann Sphere
2025 · December

Hello everybody! I just wanted to draw a clean-ish picture of the Riemann sphere projected onto the plane for trivial demonstration. Clearly, \(\infty\) is unioned with the otherwise complete \(\mathbb{C}\).

Outside of the personal influences that this experience has shown on me, this is more-or-less a running mindful abstract I come back to when Möbius maps start feeling too overwhelming!

Of course, this is a first time I attempt to draw something while utilizing several languages and packages, CSS, JavaScript, KaTeX (the JS library that allows us to translate clean HTML code into rendered LaTeX), and several other little gadgets!

riemann sphere
stereographic projection
static diagram
Formally, we think of \(\mathbb{C}_\infty = \mathbb{C} \cup \{\infty\}\) as a sphere The point \(\infty\) is the north pole in this picture
Soft colors here are just a visual cue blue lives on the sphere side green lives on the plane side and the red line is the projection path
When \(f = u + iv\) is holomorphic we get the usual Cauchy Riemann conditions \(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\) and \(\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = - \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\)
